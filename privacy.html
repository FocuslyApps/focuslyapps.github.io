This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile applications (the “Application”), provided as a free download with optional subscription plans. The Application is provided “AS IS.”

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not require users to sign in and does not store personal data on external servers. All data created in the Application is stored locally on your device. You have full control over your data and may export or import it manually where supported.

The Application may use certain third‑party services that automatically collect limited information, such as:

This information is used only for analytics, debugging, and improving app performance. The Application does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or contact details.

Location Information

The Application does not collect or use precise location data. Some third‑party analytics services may infer an approximate location based on IP address for aggregated analytics purposes only.

Third‑Party Services

The Application may use third‑party services for analytics, crash reporting, or in‑app purchases. These services may collect information used to identify you indirectly (for example, via device identifiers) and are governed by their own privacy policies. No personal user data or app content data is shared by the Application with these services.

Data Retention and Export

All application data is stored locally on your device. You may export or import your data at any time where supported. The developer does not have access to or store your personal app data.

Opt‑Out Rights

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application. You may also limit third‑party analytics collection using your device settings (for example, by disabling analytics or ad tracking).

Security

Your data is protected by your device’s security features such as encryption and screen lock. Any limited data shared with third‑party services is transmitted securely and handled according to their respective privacy policies.
